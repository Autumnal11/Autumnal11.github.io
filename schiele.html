Self-Portrait with Physalis (1912)

Egon Schiele

Period: Expressionism, Vienna Secession
Media: Unidentified
Current Location: Leopold Museum

Schiele, the prodigy son of Gustav Klimt. This piece is one of the few that reflects his own confidence in himself, the composition is set and defined, the work more realistic than most of his others, each line controlled, and his gaze matching the observer.
