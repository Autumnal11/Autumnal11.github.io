Glenn (1984)

Jean-Michel Basquiat

Period: Neo-Expressionism
Media: Acrylic, Oilstick and Xerox on Canvas
Current Location: Private Collection

Basquiat is an incredibly controversial artist, many think his following is based on pretentiousness. However, I find his use of art as a personal therapeutical outburst without defined meaning as something that should be more consistent in the art world when given grounded boundaries.
